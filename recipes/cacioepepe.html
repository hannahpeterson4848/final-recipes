Cacio e Pepe

Description

This recipe for the classic Italian dish, cacio e pepe, can't be beat! It is so creamy and flavorful - I have no doubt it'll become a household favorite.

I had this dish for the first time while on a trip in Italy and have been making it at home ever since. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 6 oz good italian pasta
  • 3 Tbsp. unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated Pecorino cheese

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a 5-quart pot, making sure to add salt to the water
  2. Cook pasta until al dente, make sure to save approximately half a cup of the pasta water
  3. Meanwhile, melt the butter in a skillet over medium heat, add the pepper and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add 1/2 cup of pasta water to the skillet and bring to a simmer
  5. Add pasta and cheese until cheese melts
  6. Transfer pasta to bowls and serve immediately
