Thai Iced Coffee

Description

This thai iced coffee is perfect in the summer or anytime you want to sit back and sip a delicious beverage. It is so simple to make at home and share with guests. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup ice
  • 2-3 tbsp condensed milk

Directions

  1. In a tall glass, add the ice and pour the chilled coffee on top
  2. Slowly add the condensed milk
  3. Sit back and enjoy!
