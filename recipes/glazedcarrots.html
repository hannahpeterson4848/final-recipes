Glazed Carrots (Yummmm!)

Description

This is a delicious recipe for glazed carrots! I recommend carrots fresh from the farmers market or your own home garden.

This recipe was made for the first time when I was eating dinner with my best friend Ivy! She loved them and can't wait to make them again! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Carrots (duh!)
  • Honey
  • Lemon juice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parsley for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Chop the carrots until they are in bite sized pieces
  2. Add the carrots to boiling water and cook until tender (about 6 minutes)
  3. Add the remaining ingredients and mix together in a saucepan over medium heat
  4. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Serve and enjoy!
